Salt-Cured Lona Kokum

Product ID: KOK-LONA-002

Our Salt-Cured Lona Kokum is a heritage preparation from the Konkan coast where kokum rinds are preserved with sea salt to develop a deep, intense tangy flavour and long shelf-life. Perfect for traditional curries, kokum water, and festive cooking.

Tradition Preserved in Every Rind

Lona Kokum has been used in coastal kitchens for generations as a souring and seasoning agent. The slow salt-curing process enhances the natural tang and adds a rich umami note, making it ideal for hearty gravies, soups, and cooling beverages.

Ingredients

  • Sun-dried kokum rind
  • Sea salt

No artificial preservatives.

Flavour & Aroma

Intensely tangy and saline with warm tropical fruit notes. The salt curing deepens kokum aroma, producing a flavour that’s richer and more complex than plain dried kokum.

How It’s Made

Fresh kokum rinds are layered with sea salt and sun-dried for several days. This natural curing method allows fermentation-like flavour development without heat or chemicals.

Usage Suggestions

  • Add to curries for deep tang
  • Use in kokum water for cooling drinks
  • Add to soups and stews for flavour depth
  • Combine with tamarind bases for gravies

Benefits

The salt-curing preserves kokum’s digestive benefits while enhancing flavour complexity, making it a kitchen staple in traditional coastal cuisine.
