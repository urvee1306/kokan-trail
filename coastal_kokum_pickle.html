Coastal Kokum Pickle

Product ID: KOK-PCL-008

A tangy and spicy coastal favourite — Coastal Kokum Pickle pairs pickled kokum rinds with mustard, chilli, and aromatic spices to create a bold condiment perfect with rice, rotis, or dal.

Authentic Recipe

Following age-old coastal recipes, this pickle uses vinegar, spices, and kokum to balance sour and spicy notes with rich aroma and shelf stability.

Ingredients

  • Dried kokum rind
  • Mustard seeds
  • Red chillies
  • Salt & spices

Usage

  • With rice and dal
  • As a side for meals
  • With sandwiches or wraps
