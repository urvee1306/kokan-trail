Tender Jackfruit Sukka (Konkani-Style Coconut Masala)

Product ID: MALVANI-ASB-001

Tender Jackfruit Sukka is a seasonal Konkani delicacy where raw jackfruit is cooked with roasted coconut masala into a dry, intensely aromatic dish. It is loved for its meaty texture and rich spice coating.

What Makes This Masala Special?

The sukka masala is dry-roasted for maximum aroma, ensuring every jackfruit piece is coated with bold coconut spice.

Ingredients

  • Tender jackfruit
  • Roasted coconut
  • Dried red chillies
  • Garlic
  • Coriander seeds

Flavour Profile

Spicy, nutty, and deeply roasted with a satisfying dry finish.

Artisan-Crafted & Hand-Roasted

Crafted using traditional slow-roasting methods to achieve intense aroma and texture.

Usage Suggestions & Pairings

  • Serve as a side with rice and dal
  • Pairs beautifully with chapati or bhakri

Health & Lifestyle Benefits

Jackfruit is rich in fibre and antioxidants, making this dish both satisfying and nutritious.
