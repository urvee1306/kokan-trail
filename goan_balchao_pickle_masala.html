Goan Balchão Pickle Masala

Product ID: GOAN-BPM-011

A robust, tangy and spicy Goan pickle masala traditionally used to prepare Balchão — a fiery prawn or fish pickle curry. With lashings of garlic, chillies, and vinegar, this blend brings coastal zest and heat to every spoonful.

What Makes This Special?

Balchão is a signature Goan pickle-curry that’s bold and rich. This masala’s balance of sourness, heat, and warm spices recreates that classic coastal flavour with depth and authenticity.

Ingredients

  • Red chillies
  • Vinegar
  • Mustard seeds
  • Garlic
  • Fenugreek

Traditional Goan recipe — no artificial flavours.

Flavour Profile

Intense tanginess up front, followed by peppery heat and a rich, savory finish — perfect for pickle curries that pair beautifully with rice.

How It’s Made

Spices and chillies are slow roasted then blended with vinegar to lock in sourness and depth. The traditional method ensures a bold yet balanced flavor profile.

Usage Suggestions & Pairings

  • Prepare Balchão with prawns or fish.
  • Add to tomato bases for tangy stew.
  • Pair with steamed rice or neer dosa.

Benefits & Lifestyle Notes

A bold choice for pickled curries that brings authentic Goan coastal taste to home cooking.
