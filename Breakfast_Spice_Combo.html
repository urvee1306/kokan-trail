Konkani Breakfast Spice Combo

Start your day with authentic coastal flavour! The Konkani Breakfast Spice Combo includes chutney powders and curry bases to make delicious breakfast staples like ghavane, neer dosa, upma, and poha with genuine Konkani taste.

Crafted for everyday morning meals, this combo gives you the flavour building blocks for traditional Konkani breakfast dishes, ensuring each bite is fragrant, balanced, and satisfying.

  • Mix with curd rice for added zest

Benefits

Brings coastal breakfast flavours to life, making meals aromatic, balanced, and easier to prepare.
