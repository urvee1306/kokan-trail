Goan Recheado Fish Curry / Paste Base

Product ID: GOA-REC-004

The Goan Recheado Base is fiery, tangy, and deeply aromatic — a signature flavour of Portuguese-influenced Goan cuisine. This ready-to-use paste delivers bold red colour, sharp spice, and vinegar-forward tang that defines authentic recheado preparations.

A Bold Goan Classic

Recheado masala is traditionally stone-ground using soaked red chillies, garlic, vinegar, and spices. Our version stays true to this heritage, creating a thick, intensely flavoured paste that can be used as both curry base and stuffing.

It is designed for those who love strong coastal flavours with a spicy kick and mouth-watering acidity.

Ingredients

  • Dried red chillies
  • Garlic
  • Goan vinegar
  • Cloves & cinnamon
  • Cumin & black pepper

Flavour Profile

  • Very spicy
  • Tangy & sharp
  • Strong garlic notes
  • Deep red colour

Usage

Use as a stuffing for pomfret, mackerel, or squid, or cook with onions and water to make a bold recheado curry. Pairs best with plain rice or bread.
