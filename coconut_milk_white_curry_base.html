Coconut Milk (White Curry) Base

Product ID: KON-WHT-005

Delicate, soothing, and fragrant — our Coconut Milk White Curry Base represents the gentler side of Konkan cuisine. Free from heavy spice and chilli heat, this curry celebrates the natural sweetness of coconut milk.

A Calm & Comforting Curry

Traditionally served to children, elders, or during recovery meals, white curry is nourishing and easy on the stomach while remaining deeply satisfying.

Ingredients

  • Fresh coconut milk
  • Green chillies (minimal)
  • Ginger
  • Cumin

Best Uses

  • Fish white curry
  • Vegetable stew
  • Prawns or clams
