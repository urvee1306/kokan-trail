Konkani Batatya Bhaji (Coconut Potato Curry)

Product ID: MALVANI-ASB-001

Konkani Batatya Bhaji is a humble yet deeply comforting coconut-based potato curry that holds a special place in everyday Konkani meals. Simple ingredients come together to create a nourishing, mildly spiced dish that is loved across generations. This curry is often served as part of a traditional rice plate, paired with dal, papad, and pickle.

What Makes This Masala Special?

The masala for Batatya Bhaji focuses on balance rather than heat. Fresh coconut is ground with gentle spices to enhance the natural sweetness of potatoes without overpowering them. This simplicity makes it both versatile and comforting.

Ingredients

  • Fresh potatoes
  • Grated coconut
  • Dried red chillies
  • Coriander seeds
  • Cumin
  • Turmeric

Flavour Profile

Mildly spiced, gently sweet, and creamy with a soft coconut finish.

Artisan-Crafted & Hand-Roasted

Spices are hand-roasted in small batches to retain aroma while ensuring the curry remains light, fragrant, and easy to digest.

Usage Suggestions & Pairings

  • Serve with steamed rice and dal
  • Pairs beautifully with chapati or poori
  • Ideal for everyday meals and simple lunches

Health & Lifestyle Benefits

Potatoes provide natural energy and potassium, while coconut adds healthy fats that support satiety and digestion. This dish is comforting, wholesome, and suitable for all age groups.
