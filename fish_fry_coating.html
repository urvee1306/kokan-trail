Konkan Fish Fry Coating Mix

Product ID: KONKAN-FFC-005

A perfectly balanced spice and coating mix designed to give your fish a crisp, flavourful crust every time you fry. Ideal for coastal fish like pomfret, bangda, surmai, and prawns.

What Makes This Special?

Blended with rice flour and aromatic spices, this mix ensures a crunchy, non-greasy finish that stays crisp even after cooking.

Ingredients / Components

  • Rice flour
  • Chilli powder
  • Turmeric
  • Garam masala
  • Salt

Food-safe, no artificial flavours or colours.

Flavour Profile

Mild heat with a savoury aromatic backbone — complements the natural flavour of fish without overpowering it.

How It’s Made / Crafted

Each serving is mixed and dried in small batches to maintain consistent particle size and spice balance for even coating and crisping.

Usage Suggestions & Pairings

  • Rub onto fish fillets or prawns before shallow frying.
  • Use with a squeeze of lemon juice for zest.
  • Pair with coconut rice or green chutney.

Benefits / Lifestyle Notes

Simplifies your frying process while keeping flavours coastal-authentic with minimal effort.
