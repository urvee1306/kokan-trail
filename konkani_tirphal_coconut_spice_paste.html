Konkani Tirphal–Coconut Spice Paste

Product ID: MALVANI-ASB-001

The Konkani Tirphal–Coconut Spice Paste is a distinctive coastal masala known for its gentle numbing aroma and citrus-like freshness derived from tirphal (Sichuan-like berries native to the Konkan coast). Combined with roasted coconut, this paste delivers an unmistakable Konkani identity to both veg and seafood curries.

What Makes This Masala Special?

Tirphal is rarely used outside traditional coastal kitchens, making this paste truly unique. Its floral, mildly tingling notes lift coconut-based gravies without adding excessive heat, resulting in elegant, well-balanced curries.

Ingredients

  • Roasted coconut
  • Tirphal (Konkan pepper berries)
  • Dried red chillies
  • Coriander seeds
  • Cumin
  • Turmeric

Flavour Profile

Aromatic, lightly spicy, citrus-floral with a smooth coconut base and subtle tingling finish.

Artisan-Crafted & Hand-Roasted

Coconut and spices are roasted separately in small batches to preserve the volatile oils of tirphal, ensuring the fragrance remains intact until cooking.

Usage Suggestions & Pairings

  • Konkani fish curry and prawns
  • Vegetable coconut curries
  • Light seafood stews

Best enjoyed with steamed rice, neer dosa, or soft chapatis.

Health & Lifestyle Benefits

Tirphal is traditionally valued for digestive support, while coconut provides healthy fats for sustained energy. Free from preservatives and artificial flavours.
