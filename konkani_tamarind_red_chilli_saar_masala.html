Konkani Tamarind–Red Chilli Masala Paste (Saar Masala)

Product ID: MALVANI-ASB-001

The Konkani Tamarind–Red Chilli Saar Masala is a tangy, warming paste traditionally used to prepare saar — a thin, flavourful soup served as part of a Konkani rice meal. Its sharp tamarind acidity and chilli warmth awaken the palate without heaviness.

What Makes This Masala Special?

This masala focuses on clarity and balance rather than richness. Tamarind provides a clean sourness while red chillies lend colour and gentle heat, making it ideal for light broths and digestive dishes.

Ingredients

  • Tamarind
  • Dried red chillies
  • Coriander seeds
  • Cumin
  • Black pepper
  • Turmeric

Flavour Profile

Tangy, warming, lightly spicy with a clean and refreshing finish.

Artisan-Crafted & Hand-Roasted

Spices are gently roasted and stone-ground to preserve natural sourness and spice aroma without bitterness.

Usage Suggestions & Pairings

  • Traditional Konkani saar
  • Light vegetable gravies
  • As a souring agent in dals

Serve hot with rice, papad, and simple vegetable sides.

Health & Lifestyle Benefits

Tamarind supports digestion and appetite, while spices stimulate metabolism. Ideal for light meals and cleansing diets.
