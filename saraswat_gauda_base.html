Saraswat / Gauda Fish Curry Base (Mild)

Product ID: SAR-FC-005

The Saraswat / Gauda Fish Curry Base represents the gentle, sattvic-inspired cooking of the Konkan Saraswat community. Mild, nourishing, and delicately spiced, this curry base is ideal for everyday meals.

Subtle & Balanced

Unlike fiery coastal curries, Saraswat-style cooking focuses on balance. Coconut forms the heart of this base, supported by minimal spices and gentle sourness from kokum or tamarind.

This curry allows the natural taste of fish to shine without overpowering heat.

Ingredients

  • Fresh coconut
  • Mild red chillies
  • Coriander & cumin
  • Turmeric
  • Kokum

Flavour Profile

  • Low spice
  • Creamy coconut base
  • Soft tang
  • Light, comforting finish

Ideal For

Pomfret, sole fish, prawns, vegetables, and light curries served with rice or boiled rice (ukdo tandul).
