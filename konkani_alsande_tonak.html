Konkani Alsande Tonak (Black-Eyed Beans Coconut Curry)

Product ID: MALVANI-ASB-001

Alsande Tonak is a protein-rich Konkani curry made using black-eyed beans simmered in a thick coconut masala. This dish is rustic, filling, and commonly prepared for hearty lunches.

What Makes This Masala Special?

The masala is roasted slightly darker to complement the earthiness of beans, creating a thick gravy that clings beautifully to each grain.

Ingredients

  • Black-eyed beans (alsande)
  • Fresh coconut
  • Dried red chillies
  • Garlic
  • Coriander seeds

Flavour Profile

Nutty, mildly spicy, and deeply satisfying with a hearty texture.

Artisan-Crafted & Hand-Roasted

Coconut and spices are roasted to medium-dark for depth without bitterness.

Usage Suggestions & Pairings

  • Serve with rice or chapati
  • Excellent as a protein-rich main course

Health & Lifestyle Benefits

Rich in plant protein and fibre, this curry supports sustained energy and satiety.
