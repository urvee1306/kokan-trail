Daily Konkan Curry Masala

Product ID: KONKAN-DCM-006

A versatile everyday spice blend rooted in Konkan kitchens, our Daily Konkan Curry Masala brings balanced flavour, warmth, and coastal aroma to all your curry bases — from fish to veg.

What Makes This Special?

Unlike one-note curry powders, this blend balances chilli, coriander, cumin and coconut for a smooth, comforting flavour that works with every meal.

Ingredients

  • Red chilli
  • Coriander seeds
  • Cumin seeds
  • Garlic
  • Dry coconut

All-natural, preservative-free.

Flavour Profile

Mild warmth with coconut sweetness and rich aroma — perfect for daily curries.

How It’s Made

Spices are roasted just enough to release aroma then ground finely for smooth integration into gravies.

Usage Suggestions & Pairings

  • Use in fish, chicken, or veg curries.
  • Works well with coconut milk or tamarind bases.
  • Pair with rice, chapati, or dosa.

Lifestyle Notes

Ideal for everyday cookery — adds authentic Konkan taste without overpowering spice.
