Goan Xacuti Coconut Masala

Product ID: GOAN-XCM-009

A deeply aromatic and complex Goan Xacuti spice blend combining roasted coconut, poppy seeds, warm whole spices, and chillies. This masala is the foundation for rich, creamy Xacuti gravies — delicious with chicken, prawn, mushroom, or vegetable Xacuti preparations.

What Makes This Special?

Xacuti is one of Goa’s signature curry styles — bold, layered, and rich. This masala’s unique character comes from its careful roast of poppy seeds and spices, which creates a thick, creamy texture when cooked.

Ingredients

  • Roasted coconut
  • Poppy seeds
  • Red chillies
  • Coriander seeds
  • Cumin seeds
  • Nutmeg & mace

Traditional Goan recipe without synthetic additives.

Flavour Profile

Deep, creamy and warm, with roasted spice richness, nutty poppy seed sweetness, and balanced chilli heat — perfect for savoury coastal curries.

How It’s Made

Ingredients are roasted in small batches to release essential oils, then ground to a silky, flavour-intense masala that forms a thick base with coconut or cream.

Usage Suggestions & Pairings

  • Add to coconut or tomato bases for Xacuti curry.
  • Great with chicken, prawns, or mushrooms.
  • Serve with rice, neer dosa, or steamed buns.

Benefits & Lifestyle Notes

A rich tropical blend that adds complexity and home-style depth to coastal gravies.
