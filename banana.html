Konkan local bananas are naturally sweet, soft-textured, and traditionally grown along the coastal belt of Maharashtra. These bananas are known for their authentic taste, high nutritional value, and everyday versatility.

What Makes This Masala Special?

Grown using traditional farming practices without artificial ripening agents, Konkan bananas develop natural sweetness, aroma, and softness unique to coastal soil and climate.

Flavour Profile

Artisan-Crafted & Hand-Roasted

Carefully harvested at the right maturity stage and ripened naturally, preserving nutrients, flavour, and freshness.

Usage Suggestions & Pairings

  • Used in traditional Konkani sweets
  • Great for kids and elders alike

Health & Lifestyle Benefits

Rich in potassium, fibre, and natural energy, bananas aid digestion, support heart health, and provide instant nourishment.
