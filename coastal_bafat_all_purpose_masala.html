Coastal Bafat All-Purpose Masala

Product ID: COASTAL-BAM-015

A versatile coastal spice blend designed to add robust flavour to a wide range of dishes. Inspired by traditional Bafat spice mixes, this all-purpose masala brings warmth, aroma, and depth to curries, stews, and stir-fries.

What Makes This Special?

Coastal Bafat masalas combine earthy chillies, aromatics, and warm spices that work equally well with veg, fish, and meat — perfect for everyday cooking.

Ingredients

  • Red chillies
  • Coriander seeds
  • Cumin seeds
  • Cloves
  • Cinnamon

All-natural blend — no artificial enhancers.

Flavour Profile

Warm and aromatic with a balance of spice and earthiness — ideal for a diversity of dishes.

How It’s Made

Ingredients are roasted lightly and blended to ensure a smooth, rounded flavour that integrates well into gravies and dry dishes alike.

Usage Suggestions & Pairings

  • Use in daily curry bases.
  • Add to soups, stews, or gravies.
  • Sprinkle on roast veggies for added warmth.

Lifestyle Notes

A go-to everyday masala for kitchens that value versatility and flavour depth.
