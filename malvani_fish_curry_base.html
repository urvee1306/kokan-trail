Malvani Fish Curry Base (Spicy)

Product ID: MAL-CB-002

Bold, fiery, and unapologetically coastal — our Malvani Fish Curry Base brings the legendary spice of Malvan straight to your kitchen. Crafted with roasted coconut, intense red chillies, and aromatic spices, this base delivers a true Malvani punch.

The Spirit of Malvan

Malvani cuisine is known for its fearless heat and deep roasted flavours. This curry base is slow-roasted to achieve a rich red colour and layered spice profile that clings beautifully to fish and seafood.

Every spoonful carries warmth, aroma, and a rustic depth that feels straight out of a coastal home kitchen.

Ingredients

  • Dry coconut (khobra)
  • Byadgi & spicy red chillies
  • Garlic
  • Coriander seeds
  • Black pepper
  • Turmeric

Flavour Profile

  • High spice level
  • Smoky roasted coconut
  • Strong aroma
  • Lingering heat

Best Used For

Surmai, bangda, prawns, shark fish, chicken rassa, and spicy vegetable curries.
