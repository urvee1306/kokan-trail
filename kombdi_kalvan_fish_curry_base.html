Kombdi / Kalvan Style Fish Curry Base

Product ID: KON-KAL-003

Our Kombdi / Kalvan Style Fish Curry Base is inspired by the everyday fish curries cooked in Konkan homes — known locally as “Kalvan”. This curry is the backbone of coastal meals, light yet flavourful, perfectly balanced to complement fresh fish without overpowering it.

True Everyday Konkan Curry

Kalvan is not festival food — it is comfort food. Cooked almost daily using seasonal fish, this curry base focuses on simplicity, freshness, and harmony of flavours. Our ready-to-eat version faithfully recreates this home-style preparation.

The base uses coconut, onion, and traditional spices in restrained proportions, making it ideal for regular consumption and light meals.

Ingredients

  • Fresh coconut
  • Onion
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Red chillies
  • Coriander & cumin
  • Tamarind extract

Flavour Profile

Mildly spicy, gently tangy, and soothingly coconut-rich. The curry allows the natural sweetness of fish like surmai, pomfret, or bangda to shine through.

Best Used With

  • Pomfret, Bangda, Paplet
  • Catla or Rohu
  • Fish fry leftovers (for next-day curry)

Everyday Comfort, Zero Effort

Ideal for busy households, this base cuts down prep time without compromising taste. Simply add fish, water, and simmer.
