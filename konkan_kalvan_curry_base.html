Konkan Kalvan Curry Base

Product ID: KONKAN-KCB-018

A homestyle curry base used daily along the Konkan coast, this blend offers a well-rounded, aromatic flavour suitable for fish, chicken, or vegetable curry preparations with coastal authenticity.

What Makes This Special?

Crafted from coastal herbs and spices, this curry base is a staple in everyday Konkan cooking. Its versatile, balanced profile integrates seamlessly with various proteins and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Coriander seeds
  • Chilli
  • Cumin
  • Garlic
  • Dry coconut

All-natural, no artificial additives.

Flavour Profile

Earthy spice warmth with coconut undertones — a gentle yet rich base for daily cooking.

How It’s Made

Spices are roasted to release essential oils, then ground finely for smooth integration into curry bases.

Usage Suggestions & Pairings

  • Use as a base for fish or veg curry.
  • Mix with coconut milk, tamarind, or kokum.
  • Serve with rice or local breads.

Lifestyle Notes

A daily staple that elevates simple meals into something fragrant and satisfying.
