Green Coconut Curry Paste

Product ID: GREEN-CCP-016

A fresh and herbaceous blend crafted with coriander leaves, grated coconut, green chillies, and cumin — this paste brings vibrant green colour and zesty, fragrant flavour to coastal curry bases.

What Makes This Special?

Ideal for those who enjoy lighter, fresh curry profiles, this paste captures the brightness of fresh herbs with creamy coconut undernotes.

Ingredients

  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Grated coconut
  • Green chillies
  • Garlic
  • Cumin seeds

Fresh and preservative-free.

Flavour Profile

Fresh, bright herbs with Coconut creaminess — mild heat and vibrant aroma.

How It’s Made

Fresh ingredients are ground into a smooth paste to retain natural colours and aromatic oils, giving an immediate flavour boost.

Usage Suggestions & Pairings

  • Use for green curry bases with veg, seafood, or chicken.
  • Serve with steamed rice or dosa.
  • Add lemon or lime for extra zing.

Lifestyle Notes

Great for clean, fresh cooking with bold herbaceous notes.
