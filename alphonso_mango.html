Alphonso Mango (Konkan Hapus Mango)

Product ID: MALVANI-ASB-001

The legendary Alphonso Mango, lovingly called Hapus, is the crown jewel of the Konkan region. Known for its rich aroma, saffron-golden flesh, and melt-in-mouth texture, this mango is celebrated worldwide as the finest table mango.

What Makes This Masala Special?

Grown in the mineral-rich laterite soil of Konkan and ripened naturally, Alphonso mangoes develop an unmatched balance of sweetness, fragrance, and creamy texture.

Each fruit is handpicked at peak maturity, ensuring superior taste, authentic flavour, and export-grade quality.

Ingredients

  • 100% Natural Alphonso Mango
  • No artificial ripeners
  • No preservatives or wax coating

Flavour Profile

Intensely sweet with floral notes, low fibre content, creamy pulp, and a lingering tropical aroma that defines the true Konkan Alphonso experience.

Artisan-Crafted & Hand-Roasted

Cultivated using traditional farming practices, Alphonso mangoes are nurtured with patience and care. Natural ripening methods preserve flavour, nutrition, and authenticity.

Usage Suggestions & Pairings

  • Enjoy fresh as a seasonal delicacy
  • Prepare aamras, milkshakes, or desserts
  • Pair with puri, chapati, or rice
  • Perfect for gifting during mango season

Health & Lifestyle Benefits

Rich in vitamins A and C, antioxidants, and natural sugars, Alphonso mangoes boost immunity, support digestion, and provide instant natural energy when consumed in moderation.
