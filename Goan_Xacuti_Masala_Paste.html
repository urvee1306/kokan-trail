Goan Xacuti Masala Paste

A legendary Goan masala known for its complex spice layering, Xacuti paste combines roasted coconut with poppy seeds and aromatic spices.

What Makes This Masala Special?

Its unique spice blend delivers warmth without excessive heat, ideal for slow-cooked curries.

Ingredients

  • Roasted coconut
  • Poppy seeds
  • Dried red chillies
  • Coriander, cloves, nutmeg

Flavour Profile

Rich, nutty, aromatic, mildly spicy.

Artisan-Crafted & Hand-Roasted

Spices are roasted individually to preserve their unique aromas.

Usage Suggestions & Pairings

  • Chicken Xacuti
  • Vegetable Xacuti

Health & Lifestyle Benefits

Spice-rich paste aids metabolism and enhances nutrient absorption.
