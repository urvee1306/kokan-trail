Goan Xacuti Fish Curry Base

Product ID: GOA-XAC-003

Xacuti is one of Goa’s most celebrated curries — rich, aromatic, and deeply complex. Our Goan Xacuti Fish Curry Base recreates this iconic dish using roasted coconut, poppy seeds, and a carefully balanced spice blend.

Royal Goan Flavours

Traditionally served at festive meals, Xacuti is known for its deep brown colour and layered flavour. This base is slow-roasted to unlock nutty aromas and warm spice notes that develop beautifully as the curry simmers.

Ingredients

  • Roasted coconut
  • Poppy seeds
  • Dried red chillies
  • Cloves, cinnamon & nutmeg
  • Coriander & cumin

Flavour Profile

  • Medium spice
  • Nutty and aromatic
  • Rich and creamy texture
  • Slow-building warmth

Best Paired With

Pomfret, basa, crab, chicken, or vegetables. Serve with rice or Goan poi.
