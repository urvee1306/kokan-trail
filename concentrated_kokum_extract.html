Concentrated Kokum Extract

Product ID: KOK-EXT-005

Our Concentrated Kokum Extract is made by gently reducing fresh kokum pulp to a thick, aromatic, richly flavoured base that enhances curries, gravies, and beverages. It’s a versatile kitchen staple for authentic coastal cooking.

Purely Extracted, Deeply Flavoured

Crafted through slow reduction, this extract locks in deep colour, vibrant tang, and complex kokum notes — perfect for dishes that need both acidity and depth without altering texture.

Ingredients

  • Fresh kokum fruit pulp

No added sugar, preservatives or artificial enhancers.

How It’s Used

  • Add to fish, prawn, or vegetable curries
  • Mix in kokum gravies for natural tang
  • Blend into solkadhi or kokum drinks

Benefits

Rich in antioxidants, this extract improves digestion, supports metabolism, and adds authentic coastal flavour with minimal effort.
