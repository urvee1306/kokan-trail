Kolambi / Prawn Curry Base

Product ID: KON-KOL-002

Bring home the irresistible aroma of coastal seafood with our Kolambi Prawn Curry Base. Crafted to enhance the natural sweetness of prawns, this ready-to-eat base balances coconut richness, spice heat, and gentle tang — just like home-cooked Malvani and Goan-style prawn curries.

Inspired by Coastal Homes

In Konkan households, prawn curry is a celebration dish — cooked slowly, spiced thoughtfully, and always aromatic. This base captures that tradition by blending roasted coconut, onions, and spices in proportions perfected over generations.

Ingredients

  • Fresh coconut
  • Onion
  • Ginger & garlic
  • Red chillies
  • Coriander & cumin
  • Tamarind

Flavour Experience

Creamy and spicy with mild tanginess, this base coats prawns beautifully without overpowering their delicate flavour.

How to Use

  • Sauté prawns lightly in oil
  • Add curry base + water
  • Simmer for 5–7 minutes

Best enjoyed with steamed rice or neer dosa.
