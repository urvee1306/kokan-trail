Ghati Masala Fish Curry Base

Product ID: KON-GHA-004

Bold, rustic, and intensely aromatic — our Ghati Masala Fish Curry Base is inspired by the Ghats region where spicy gravies reign supreme. This curry base delivers deep roasted flavours with unmistakable heat.

From the Hills to the Coast

Ghati masala bridges inland spice traditions with coastal seafood. The result is a thicker, darker, and spicier curry that pairs beautifully with firm fish and hearty grains.

Ingredients

  • Roasted coconut
  • Ghati masala spices
  • Onion & garlic
  • Red chillies
  • Black pepper

Heat & Depth

This base offers layered spice — warming heat upfront, followed by smoky coconut and earthy spice undertones that linger long after the meal.

Recommended Pairings

  • Surmai steaks
  • Catfish
  • Shellfish
