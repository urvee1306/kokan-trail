Triphal Coastal Aromatic Mix

Product ID: TRIPHAL-CAM-019

A rare aromatic mix inspired by Saraswat and Goan coastal traditions — featuring triphal (a regional peppercorn), coconut, and gently warming spices. Adds a citrus-pepper aroma to fish, vegetable, or coconut-based gravies.

What Makes This Special?

Triphal (a regional pepper berry) lends a unique citrus-pepper aroma not found in ordinary spice mixes. When combined with coastal coconut and aromatics, it creates a flavour that’s distinctively coastal and complex.

Ingredients

  • Triphal peppercorns
  • Coconut
  • Green chilli
  • Coriander seeds

All-natural, handcrafted.

Flavour Profile

Citrusy pepper spice with gentle warmth, balanced by creamy coconut — perfect for coastal gravies and aromatic bases.

How It’s Made

Triphal berries and spices are roasted lightly before grinding with coconut to preserve their aroma and unique fragrance.

Usage Suggestions & Pairings

  • Add to fish or vegetable curries for an aromatic boost.
  • Use with coconut or tamarind bases.
  • Pair with rice or soft dosas.

Lifestyle Notes

Perfect for those who enjoy unique regional flavours with complexity.
