Mangalorean Gassi Masala

Product ID: MANGALORE-GMM-014

A signature spice blend from the Mangalore coast, crafted for hearty gassi curries. Coconut, red chillies, and fenugreek come together for a rich, layered flavour that pairs beautifully with chicken, fish, or mixed vegetables.

What Makes This Special?

This is the classic spice base used in traditional Mangalorean curries — bold, coconut-rich, and aromatic — capturing local culinary heritage.

Ingredients

  • Coconut
  • Red chillies
  • Fenugreek seeds
  • Garlic
  • Cumin

Natural, no artificial preservatives.

Flavour Profile

Warm and earthy with coconut sweetness and balanced chilli heat — perfect for rich gravies.

How It’s Made

Ingredients are roasted just enough to unlock their oils, then ground for a smooth, aromatic finish.

Usage Suggestions & Pairings

  • Add to coconut or tamarind bases for gassi curries.
  • Pair with rice, chapati, or neer dosa.
  • Goes well with prawns, chicken, or mixed veg.

Lifestyle Notes

Great for meals with rich, heritage coastal flavour.
