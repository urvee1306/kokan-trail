Ghavan Coconut Curry Mix

Product ID: KONKAN-GCCM-007

A mild, creamy coconut-based curry mix specifically crafted to pair with Ghavan — the thin rice crepe beloved in coastal Konkani homes. This mix combines fresh coconut with gentle spices to create a soothing, fragrant curry ideal with soft ghavan or rice.

What Makes This Special?

Traditional coastal methods emphasize freshness and balance — this curry mix uses freshly dried coconut and light aromatics to create a smooth, rich gravy base that complements delicate meals like ghavan, neer dosa, or steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • Fresh coconut powder
  • Green chilli
  • Cumin seeds
  • Coriander seeds
  • Fenugreek

All-natural and preservative-free.

Flavour Profile

Creamy and mild with soft coconut sweetness, gentle spice warmth, and subtle aromatic notes — a perfect accompaniment to light coastal breads.

How It’s Made

The blend is crafted from freshly dried coconut and light spices roasted just enough to release their aroma — preserving a tender, natural flavour that enhances delicate dishes.

Usage Suggestions & Pairings

  • Prepare the curry base for Ghavan or neer dosa.
  • Serve with steamed rice or soft dosas.
  • Add a squeeze of lime for tangy brightness.

Benefits & Lifestyle Notes

Mild and easy to digest, this mix aligns with lighter, everyday meals — ideal for kids, elders, and anyone seeking soothing, wholesome curry.
