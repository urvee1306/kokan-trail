Prawn Curry Special Masala

Product ID: PRAWN-PCS-017

Specially curated for prawn lovers, this masala blend enhances prawns’ natural sweetness with balanced coastal spices and a touch of coconut richness. A favourite for traditional prawn curries with deep aroma and flavour.

What Makes This Special?

This masala is designed to complement delicate seafood, especially prawns, without overpowering their fresh taste. The spice profile balances warmth, coconut depth, and mild heat.

Ingredients

  • Dry coconut
  • Red chillies
  • Coriander seeds
  • Cumin seeds
  • Peppercorns

Hand-crafted and preservative-free.

Flavour Profile

Subtle sweetness from coconut, gentle chilli warmth, and aromatic balance — ideal for seafood bases.

How It’s Made

Spices and coconut are roasted and ground to preserve essential oils, yielding a smooth, rich masala that integrates perfectly in prawn gravies.

Usage Suggestions & Pairings

  • Use in prawn gravy with coconut or tamarind bases.
  • Pair with fluffy rice or neer dosa.
  • Add lime juice before serving for brightness.

Lifestyle Notes

Perfect for seafood enthusiasts seeking authentic coastal heat and aroma.
