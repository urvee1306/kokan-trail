Kokum & Kadhi Spice Combo

A harmonious blend of dried kokum and instant kadhi masala designed for those who love tangy, refreshing kadhi and digestive drinks. This combo brings the true taste of coastal kadhi and solkadhi into your kitchen.

Combo Components

This combo pairs naturally tangy kokum with a balanced kadhi spice mix containing mild aromatics, ensuring an easy-to-prepare, flavourful coastal kadhi or digestive drink.

Ingredients

  • Dried kokum
  • Instant kadhi masala blend

All-natural ingredients, no artificial additives.

  • Great as an everyday digestive tonic.

Benefits

Rich in antioxidants and digestive agents, this combo supports gut health while adding the coastal tang you love.
