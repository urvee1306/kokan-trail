Kundapur Red Curry Masala

Product ID: KUNDAPUR-RCM-012

Rich red curry masala hailing from Kundapur’s coastal kitchens. This blend brings vibrant colour and layered flavour to fish, crab, chicken, or mixed vegetable curries with bold coastal character.

What Makes This Special?

Kundapur cuisine is known for its deep red curry bases — bright, spicy, and richly aromatic. This masala captures that profile with careful spice balance.

Ingredients

  • Byadgi chillies
  • Coriander seeds
  • Cumin seeds
  • Garlic
  • Turmeric

All-natural and preservative-free.

Flavour Profile

Bright red colour with medium heat, deep spice warmth, and tropical coconut undertones.

How It’s Made

Fresh spices are roasted and ground in traditional proportions to deliver vibrant colour and aroma.

Usage Suggestions & Pairings

  • Add to coconut-tamarind fish gravies.
  • Use with chicken or crab curries.
  • Serve with rice, chapati, or neer dosa.

Lifestyle Notes

A versatile spice base for colourful, bold curries with coastal flair.
