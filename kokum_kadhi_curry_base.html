Kokum Kadhi Style Curry Base

Product ID: KON-KOK-006

Refreshing, lightly spiced, and beautifully pink — our Kokum Kadhi Style Curry Base is a soothing Konkan classic known for aiding digestion and cooling the body.

Kokum kadhi is traditionally served after meals or alongside rice during hot summers. This base simplifies preparation while keeping flavours authentic.
