Konkan Kala Roasted Masala

Product ID: KONKAN-KRM-003

A dark, smoky and deeply aromatic spice blend that brings authentic coastal richness to your curries and gravies. Perfect for chicken, mutton, and hearty vegetable dishes that need bold depth.

What Makes This Special?

This masala is roasted longer than typical blends to extract intense roasted flavours from coconut, spices, and aromatics. The result is a bold, earthy spice profile unlike ordinary curry powders.

Ingredients / Components

  • Charred dry coconut
  • Peppercorns
  • Cloves
  • Cinnamon
  • Jeera (cumin)
  • Stone flower (dagad phool)

100% natural, no preservatives or artificial colours.

Flavour Profile

Bold roasted aroma with warm pepper spice, earthy coconut undertones and a lingering savoury finish — ideal for hearty gravies.

How It’s Made / Crafted

Whole spices and dry coconut are slow roasted over low heat by hand, then ground to bring out deep, smoky richness with every use.

Usage Suggestions & Pairings

  • Use 2–3 tbsp in chicken or mutton curry base.
  • Add to roasted vegetables for bold flavour.
  • Great with rice, bhakri, or rustic breads.

Benefits / Lifestyle Notes

Roasted coconut and whole spices provide antioxidant properties and robust flavour without the need for heavy oil or artificial enhancers.
