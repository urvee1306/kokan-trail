Traditional Malvani Masala Paste

Product ID: MALVANI-ASB-001

Our Traditional Malvani Masala Paste is the backbone of iconic coastal curries. Prepared using roasted coconut, red chillies, and whole spices, this paste delivers the unmistakable depth, aroma, and warmth of Malvani home cooking.

What Makes This Masala Special?

Unlike dry powders, this paste captures the oils and richness released during slow roasting. It recreates the exact texture and taste used by Malvani households, eliminating the need for grinding fresh masala every time.

Ingredients

  • Dry coconut (khobra)
  • Byadgi & spicy red chillies
  • Coriander seeds
  • Cumin
  • Black pepper
  • Cloves & cinnamon

Flavour Profile

Bold, deeply roasted, spicy with a lingering coconut finish.

Artisan-Crafted & Hand-Roasted

Each batch is roasted slowly and stone-ground to retain authentic flavour, colour, and aroma without shortcuts.

Usage Suggestions & Pairings

  • Malvani fish curry
  • Chicken rassa or mutton curry
  • Vegetable usal

Health & Lifestyle Benefits

Free from preservatives, rich in natural fats and antioxidants that support digestion and sustained energy.
