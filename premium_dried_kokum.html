Premium Dried Kokum (Amsul)

Product ID: KOK-AMSL-001

Our Premium Dried Kokum (Amsul) is sun-dried using traditional Konkan methods to preserve its deep purple colour, natural sourness, and cooling properties.

Authentic Konkan Sour Agent

Kokum has been the backbone of coastal cooking for centuries. Unlike tamarind, kokum adds a gentle, aromatic sourness without overpowering the dish. Each rind is carefully cleaned, salted lightly, and sun-dried to retain maximum flavour and nutrition.

Ingredients

  • 100% natural dried kokum rind

No preservatives, no artificial colouring.

Uses

  • Fish & prawn curries
  • Solkadhi & kokum saar
  • Vegetable gravies
  • Summer cooling drinks

Health Benefits

Kokum aids digestion, reduces acidity, and has cooling properties that help balance body heat, especially in coastal climates.
