Traditional Konkani Veg Kolombo Curry

Product ID: MALVANI-ASB-001

Konkani Veg Kolombo is a soulful, everyday curry from Konkan households, known for its balanced spice, gentle coconut base, and comforting warmth. This curry is often prepared with seasonal vegetables and served as part of a simple yet deeply satisfying home meal.

What Makes This Masala Special?

Kolombo is not festive food — it is daily nourishment. Our ready-to-use base is crafted to retain that humble authenticity, combining roasted coconut and mild spices without overpowering vegetables. It is light, aromatic, and versatile.

Ingredients

  • Fresh coconut
  • Coriander seeds
  • Red chillies
  • Cumin
  • Turmeric
  • Tamarind

Flavour Profile

Mildly spicy, gently tangy, and coconut-forward. The flavours are soft and rounded, making it suitable for all age groups.

Artisan-Crafted & Hand-Roasted

Each spice is hand-roasted in small batches to release natural oils while maintaining a light, digestible character.

Usage Suggestions & Pairings

  • Mixed vegetables (pumpkin, drumstick, potato)
  • Serve with rice or soft chapatis

Health & Lifestyle Benefits

Light coconut gravies are known to be easy on digestion and suitable for everyday meals without heaviness.
