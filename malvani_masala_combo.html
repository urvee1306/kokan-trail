Malvani Masala Combo (Hot + Mild)

Product ID: MMC-001

Discover the authentic taste of Malvan with our Malvani Masala Combo — featuring two handcrafted spice blends: the fiery Hot Malvani Masala and the aromatic Mild Masala. Perfectly curated to suit every palate, this combo brings the rich coastal flavours of Maharashtra into your kitchen.

About This Combo

Malvan cuisine is known for its bold, robust flavours derived from sun-dried chillies, roasted coconut, and traditional coastal spices. This combo gives you the best of both worlds — the spicy kick of the Hot variant and the balanced, fragrant Mild version — to elevate everything from seafood curries to vegetable gravies.

Whether you're cooking for spice lovers or those who prefer gentle warmth, this pairing ensures you never compromise on authentic taste.

Ingredients

  • Sun-dried chillies
  • Roasted coconut
  • Coriander seeds
  • Black pepper
  • Cumin seeds
  • Turmeric
  • Cloves & cinnamon

100% natural, no artificial colours, no preservatives.

Flavour Profile

Hot Variant: Deep red hue, bold chilli heat, layered spice notes. Mild Variant: Warm colours, aromatic coconut undertones with subtle warmth.

How It’s Made

Traditional methods are used to roast and grind these spices in small batches, preserving their essential oils and natural aromas. The result is a full, authentic spice profile that instantly transforms any dish.

Usage Suggestions

  • Seafood Curries: Add 2–3 tbsp to coconut bases for an authentic coastal curry.
  • Chicken / Mutton: Use as a marinade or in thick gravies.
  • Vegetable Gravies: Add depth and coastal aroma.
  • Stir Fries & Usal: Sprinkle for an added punch.

Benefits

Packed with natural antioxidants from roasted coconut and spices, these blends enhance flavour while supporting digestion and overall sensory delight.
