Konkani Veg Kurma (Coconut & Poppy Seeds)

Product ID: MALVANI-ASB-001

Konkani Veg Kurma is a delicately spiced, creamy curry known for its luxurious texture and subtle sweetness. Enriched with coconut and poppy seeds (khus-khus), this curry is traditionally prepared for special meals and celebratory lunches in Konkani households.

What Makes This Masala Special?

The inclusion of poppy seeds sets this kurma apart. They add a natural creaminess without heaviness, creating a smooth, refined gravy that gently coats vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Fresh coconut
  • Poppy seeds (khus-khus)
  • Green chillies
  • Coriander seeds
  • Cumin

Flavour Profile

Mild, creamy, aromatic, and slightly sweet with a soothing finish.

Artisan-Crafted & Hand-Roasted

Coconut and spices are lightly roasted to preserve colour, aroma, and nutritional value.

Usage Suggestions & Pairings

  • Mixed vegetables or paneer
  • Best served with rice, appam, or soft chapatis

Health & Lifestyle Benefits

Poppy seeds are rich in minerals and healthy fats, making this curry nourishing yet gentle.
