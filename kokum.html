Kokum (Konkan Amsul)

Product ID: MALVANI-ASB-001

Kokum is a tangy, deep-purple fruit used extensively in Konkani and Malvani cuisine for its cooling properties.

What Makes This Masala Special?

Naturally sun-dried kokum rinds with intense colour, flavour, and medicinal value.

Ingredients

  • Sun-dried Kokum Rind

Flavour Profile

Tangy, mildly sour, refreshing with earthy undertones.

Artisan-Crafted & Hand-Roasted

Traditionally dried under the Konkan sun to lock flavour and nutrients.

Usage Suggestions & Pairings

  • Kokum saar & solkadhi
  • Curries & dals
  • Cooling summer drinks

Health & Lifestyle Benefits

Known for digestive, cooling, and anti-inflammatory properties.
