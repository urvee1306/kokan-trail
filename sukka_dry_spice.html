Konkan Sukka Dry Spice Mix

Product ID: KONKAN-SDM-004

Experience the authentic taste of coastal Konkan with our Konkan Sukka Dry Spice Mix — a specially crafted blend that brings the bold flavours of roasted coconut, chilies, and traditional spices into your dry gravies, sukka dishes, and rustic curries. Perfect for seafood, chicken, or vegetarian sukka preparations.

What Makes This Special?

Konkan Sukka Dry Spice Mix captures the essence of coastal roasting techniques where spices and coconut are slow roasted to bring out deep aroma and a rich, smoky profile. This dry mix elevates every dish with its well-balanced heat and earthy depth.

Ingredients

  • Dry coconut (khobra)
  • Red chillies
  • Garlic
  • Coriander seeds
  • Fenugreek
  • Cumin

100% natural, no artificial colours or preservatives.

Flavour Profile

A robust, earthy base with subtle spice warmth, fragrant roasted coconut notes, and a lingering finish — ideal for dry stir-fries, sukka, and spiced gravies.

How It’s Made

Small batches of coconut and spices are dry roasted by expert masala makers, then blended to maintain optimal flavour complexity without overpowering heat.

Usage Suggestions & Pairings

  • Use in prawn sukka or chicken sukka preparations.
  • Sprinkle into dry vegetable stir-fries for extra flavour.
  • Combine with coconut oil and curry leaves for classic Konkan dry gravies.

Health & Lifestyle Benefits

Natural dry spices like cumin, coriander, and coconut support digestion and metabolism. This mix is free from fillers and additives, perfect for traditional clean cooking.
