Handcrafted Konkan Bamboo Basket (Tokri)

Product ID: MALVANI-ASB-001

The Konkan Bamboo Tokri is a timeless household essential, handwoven by rural artisans using age-old bamboo weaving techniques. Traditionally used for carrying grains, vegetables, flowers, and daily essentials, this basket reflects the simplicity, durability, and sustainability of Konkan village life.

What Makes This Product Special?

Each tokri is entirely handcrafted without machines. The tight weave ensures strength, while the natural bamboo finish preserves its rustic charm.

Materials Used

  • Traditional hand-weaving techniques

Artisan-Crafted & Eco-Friendly

Made by Konkan artisans using renewable bamboo, supporting rural livelihoods and sustainable craftsmanship.

Usage Suggestions

  • Home décor accent
