Malvani Tikhla Fish Curry Mix

Product ID: MALVANI-TFM-002

Bring the true taste of the Konkan coast to your kitchen with our Malvani Tikhla Fish Curry Mix. Crafted using freshly roasted spices and traditional coastal ingredients, this mix simplifies the creation of an authentic Malvani fish curry — rich, tangy, spicy, and deeply aromatic.

What Makes This Curry Mix Authentic?

This is more than a spice blend — it’s a heritage flavour profile passed down through generations of Malvani cooks. Featuring a balanced combination of roasted coconut, Kashmiri chillies, fenugreek, and coriander seeds, this mix evokes the bold, slightly tangy savour of coastal fish curries without overpowering heat.

Unlike off-the-shelf powders, our Tikhla mix uses carefully selected ingredients and traditional roasting methods to deliver deep, layered flavour and colour.

Ingredients

  • Dry coconut (khobra)
  • Kashmiri chillies
  • Fenugreek seeds
  • Coriander seeds
  • Turmeric
  • Mustard seeds
  • Cumin

All natural — no preservatives, no artificial colours.

Flavour Profile

The Malvani Tikhla Fish Curry Mix delivers:

  • Aromatic base: roasted coconut sweetness
  • Savoury depth: coriander & fenugreek notes
  • Warm heat: mild-medium chilli warmth
  • Tangy finish: perfectly suited for coastal fish

Hand-Roasted & Traditional

Each batch is hand-crafted by coastal spice artisans who roast the base ingredients to unlock their essential oils, preserving both aroma and flavour depth. The careful roast ensures the curry mix integrates beautifully with coconut, tamarind, or kokum bases — whichever you prefer.

Usage Suggestions & Pairings

  • Classic Malvani Fish Curry: Combine with coconut-tamarind gravy for a rich, authentic dish.
  • Kingfish, Pomfret & Bangda: Works perfectly with all coastal fish varieties.
  • Prawns & Seafood: Adds balanced heat to prawn curry bases.
  • Vegetable Gravies: Also great with mixed veg curry for a coastal twist.

Pair with steamed rice, neer dosa, ghee rice, appam, or chapati for a full coastal experience.

Health & Lifestyle Benefits

Traditional spices like turmeric and fenugreek have known antioxidant and digestive benefits. This mix avoids synthetics, embracing clean, natural, wholesome ingredients — perfect for mindful home cooking.
