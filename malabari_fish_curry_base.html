Malabari Fish Curry Base (Kerala Coastal)

Product ID: MALB-FC-006

Inspired by Kerala’s legendary coastal kitchens, our Malabari Fish Curry Base delivers creamy coconut richness balanced with gentle spice and subtle sourness.

Kerala’s Coastal Comfort

Malabari fish curry is known for its orange-red coconut gravy infused with curry leaves and mild spices. This base recreates that authentic homestyle flavour without overpowering heat.

Ingredients

  • Coconut paste
  • Kashmiri red chillies
  • Turmeric
  • Curry leaves
  • Tamarind / kodampuli

Flavour Profile

  • Medium spice
  • Creamy coconut body
  • Gentle sour finish
  • Warm aromatic notes

Serving Suggestions

Best paired with seer fish, sardines, or prawns. Serve with steamed rice, appam, or Kerala matta rice.
