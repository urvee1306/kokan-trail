Tikhla Style Konkani Curry Base (Non-Coconut)

Product ID: KON-TIK-001

Experience the bold simplicity of traditional Konkani kitchens with our Tikhla Style Konkani Curry Base. Unlike coconut-heavy gravies, this distinctive non-coconut base celebrates roasted spices, onion, garlic, and tangy notes that define everyday home cooking along the Konkan coast.

A Forgotten Coastal Classic

Tikhla curry is a humble yet deeply flavourful preparation traditionally made on days when coconut was unavailable or intentionally avoided. This curry base reflects that philosophy — simple ingredients, carefully roasted, blended into a thick, aromatic paste that delivers intense flavour without richness overload.

Our ready-to-eat version preserves the rustic character of authentic Tikhla curries while making it effortless for modern kitchens. Just open, temper, and add your choice of protein or vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Dried red chillies
  • Coriander seeds
  • Cumin
  • Tamarind
  • Konkan spices

No coconut. No preservatives. No artificial flavours.

Flavour Profile

Robust, spicy, and lightly tangy — this curry base offers a clean heat with deep roasted notes. The absence of coconut allows spices and tamarind to shine, making it ideal for those who prefer lighter yet bold gravies.

Perfect Pairings

  • Clams (Tisrya / Kalwa)
  • Dry fish (Sukat, Bombil)
  • Egg curry
  • Potato or ridge gourd sabzi

Serve with boiled rice, ukda rice, or bhakri for a truly traditional meal.

Why You’ll Love It

This base is ideal for everyday cooking — lighter on the stomach, quicker to prepare, yet packed with nostalgic Konkani flavours. Perfect for weekday meals and fasting-day variations where coconut is avoided.
