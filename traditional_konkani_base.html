Traditional Konkani Style Curry Base

Product ID: KKM-CB-001

Experience the soul of the Konkan coast with our Traditional Konkani Style Curry Base. This ready-to-cook base is inspired by home kitchens of Goa, coastal Maharashtra, and Karnataka — where slow-cooked coconut gravies form the heart of everyday meals.

Authentic Coastal Heritage

Konkani cuisine is known for its delicate balance — gentle spice, coconut richness, and natural souring agents like kokum or tamarind. This curry base is crafted using traditional grinding and roasting techniques to preserve that balance.

Unlike commercial gravies, this base does not overpower your dish. Instead, it enhances the natural flavour of fish, vegetables, or lentils, making it ideal for everyday home cooking.

Ingredients

  • Fresh coconut & roasted coconut
  • Coriander seeds
  • Byadgi red chillies
  • Cumin & black pepper
  • Turmeric
  • Tamarind / kokum

No preservatives • No artificial colour • Home-style recipe

Flavour Profile

  • Mild to medium spice
  • Natural coconut sweetness
  • Light tangy finish
  • Balanced, comforting taste

How to Use

  • Add oil and sauté onions
  • Add the curry base and cook for 3–4 minutes
  • Add fish, vegetables, or lentils
  • Add water and simmer

Best enjoyed with steamed rice, idli, dosa, or neer dosa.

Ideal For

Fish curry, vegetable curry, dal curry, mixed seafood curry, and traditional everyday meals.
