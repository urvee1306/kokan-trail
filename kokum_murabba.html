Kokum Murabba (Sweet Preserve)

Product ID: KOK-MRB-007

A sweet and tangy preserve made by cooking kokum rinds with jaggery and mild spices. Kokum Murabba brings the perfect balance of sweet and sour — great with rice, curd, rotis or snacks.

Tradition & Taste

Murabba is a cherished condiment across Indian households. Our kokum version adds coastal flavour complexity while keeping the sweet preserve soul of tradition intact.

Ingredients

  • Dried kokum
  • Jaggery
  • Mild spices

No synthetic colour, preservative-free.

Usage

  • Enjoy with curd rice
  • Spread on toast or chapati
  • Pair with desserts or snacks
