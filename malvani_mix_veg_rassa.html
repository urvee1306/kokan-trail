Malvani Mix Veg Rassa (Coconut Curry)

Product ID: MALVANI-ASB-001

Malvani Mix Veg Rassa brings bold coastal flavours to vegetarian cooking. Known for its deeper spice and roasted coconut character, this rassa is inspired by restaurant-style Malvani gravies traditionally paired with bhakri and rice.

What Makes This Masala Special?

The masala is darker, richer, and more aromatic due to extended roasting, delivering a robust Malvani experience without overpowering vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Roasted coconut
  • Byadgi chillies
  • Coriander seeds
  • Garlic
  • Onion

Flavour Profile

Bold, spicy, smoky, and deeply satisfying with lingering warmth.

Artisan-Crafted & Hand-Roasted

Crafted in small batches using traditional Malvani roasting techniques.

Usage Suggestions & Pairings

  • Mixed vegetables or paneer
  • Serve with bhakri or steamed rice

Health & Lifestyle Benefits

Spices used are known to boost digestion and metabolism.
