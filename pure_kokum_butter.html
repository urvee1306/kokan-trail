Pure Kokum Butter

Product ID: KOK-BUT-001

Pure Kokum Butter is a premium, all-natural butter extracted from kokum seeds using cold-press methods. Known for its rich nutritional profile and deep moisturising properties, this butter is perfect for both culinary and skincare uses.

What Makes This Special?

Kokum butter has a firm texture that melts easily upon contact with skin, leaving it deeply moisturised without greasiness. It has a subtle nutty aroma and is prized in traditional coastal households for its versatility — from enriching curries to nourishing skin and hair.

Ingredients

  • 100% pure kokum seed butter

No additives, no chemicals, no artificial fragrances.

Flavour & Texture (Culinary)

Mildly nutty with smooth consistency — ideal for traditional sweets, desserts, and specialised rustic gravies where a delicate richness is desired.

How It’s Made

Kokum seeds are gently cold-pressed to extract the butter, ensuring full retention of natural fatty acids, antioxidants, and flavour compounds. No heat or solvents are used during processing.

Usage Suggestions

  • Skincare: Use as a body butter, lip balm base, or dry skin treatment.
  • Haircare: Apply on hair ends or scalp for hydration.
  • Culinary: Add in small amounts to curries or sweets for a creamy finish.

Benefits

Kokum butter is highly emollient, rich in antioxidants, and aids in soothing dry, irritated skin. Culinary use brings subtle depth without overpowering other flavours.
