Karwar Coconut Curry Mix

Product ID: KARWAR-CCM-013

A gentle, coconut-forward curry mix from the Karwar coast. This blend lends a smooth, sweet coconut base with mild spice heat — perfect for fish, chicken, or vegetable curries that call for soothing yet rich flavours.

What Makes This Special?

Karwar cuisine emphasises subtle balance — this mix blends mellow coconut richness with mild spice warmth for a smooth, enjoyable curry base.

Ingredients

  • Coconut
  • Coriander seeds
  • Garlic
  • Red chillies
  • Cumin

100% natural, preservative-free.

Flavour Profile

Creamy coconut sweetness balanced with mild spice warmth — a soothing but flavourful base.

How It’s Made

Fresh coconut and spices are roasted lightly to retain subtle aromatics, then ground for a smooth texture.

Usage Suggestions & Pairings

  • Use in fish or vegetable curries.
  • Serve with rice, dosa, or chapati.
  • Add lime or tamarind for tangy contrast.

Lifestyle Notes

Great for mild everyday meals and family-friendly cooking.
